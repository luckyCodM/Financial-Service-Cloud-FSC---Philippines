Understanding the Salesforce Partner Tiers

Salesforce evaluates and recognizes its partners based on their performance, certifications, innovation and client success. These tiers represent a partner’s growth, expertise and impact within the Salesforce ecosystem.

There are 4 partner tiers of Salesforce:

  • Base Partner (Previously Registered Partner)- It’s the entry stage for partners starting their Salesforce journey. They start building their foundational expertise and their first successful implementations.
  • Ridge Partner (Previously Silver Partner)- When an organization like CodM Software demonstrates experience and a track record of successful Salesforce projects, it is recognized with the Ridge Consulting Partner badge for its consistent delivery, certified experts, and strong customer satisfaction.
  • Crest Partner (Previously Gold Partner)- This badge is awarded to partners with advanced Salesforce expertise, large-scale project delivery, and measurable business impact. Crest Partners are trusted by enterprise-level organizations worldwide.
  • Summit Partner (Previously Platinum Partner)- It is the highest and most prestigious recognition in the Salesforce ecosystem and it is given to global partners demonstrating exceptional innovation, customer success, and leadership qualities.

Becoming a Salesforce Ridge Consulting Partner puts CodM Software among Salesforce’s recognized and trusted consulting partners, validating our capabilities, experience, and commitment to excellence.
