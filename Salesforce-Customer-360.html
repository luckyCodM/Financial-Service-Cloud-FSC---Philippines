What Is Salesforce Customer 360 Degree

Salesforce Customer 360 degree is not a single product, but a connected approach to CRM that unifies customer data across the Salesforce ecosystem. It removes data silos and helps businesses see, understand and engage customers more effectively. This results in better experiences and stronger long-term relationships.

  • Data Unification :- It provides end-to-end setup covering admissions, student management, communication processes and faculty workflows, all tailored to your institution.
